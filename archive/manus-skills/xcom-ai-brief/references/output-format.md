# X.com Brief — Output Format Reference

Use these exact section templates when writing the weekly X.com AI Intelligence Brief.

## Header

```markdown
# Weekly X.com AI Intelligence Brief
## Week of [Date Range] | Curated for AI & Innovation Leaders

> **About this brief:** This is a weekly X.com (Twitter) AI intelligence brief sourced entirely from X.com discourse, announcements, and threads. It is written for Bill — an AI and innovation leader at a publicly traded company — who currently uses Manus, OpenClaw, Perplexity Computer, Claude Code, Claude Desktop, Antigravity, and NotebookLM. Relevance scores (1–10) weight practical applicability, recency, source credibility, fit with Bill's tool stack, and uniqueness of signal.

---
```

## Section 1: Executive Summary — Top 5 Things Bill Needs to Know This Week

```markdown
## 1. Executive Summary — Top 5 Things Bill Needs to Know This Week
```

Write exactly 5 numbered paragraphs. Each: bold opening sentence, 3-5 sentences of analysis, bold actionable recommendation.

```markdown
**1. [Signal headline].** [3-5 sentences of analysis explaining what happened, why it matters, and the implications for enterprise AI leaders.] **Bill should [specific action] this week.**
```

## Section 2: Top Picks — Highest-Scored Posts & Threads

```markdown
## 2. Top Picks — Highest-Scored Posts & Threads
```

Feature the **top 5 highest-scoring posts/threads** (minimum score: 8.0/10):

```markdown
### Pick #[N] — [Author Name] (@handle) on [Topic]
**Relevance Score: [X.X]/10**

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Author** | [Full Name] (@handle) |
| **Date** | [Date] |
| **X.com Links** | [Direct URL](URL) (may include multiple links) |
| **Score Breakdown** | Practical applicability: X · Recency: X · Source credibility: X · Tool stack fit: X · Uniqueness: X |

**What [Author] Said:** [Direct quotes and paraphrased content. Use italics for direct quotes.]

**Key Takeaways:**
- [Takeaway 1]
- [Takeaway 2]
- [Takeaway 3]

**Suggested Actions for Bill:**
1. [Specific actionable recommendation.]
2. [Second recommendation.]
3. [Third recommendation if warranted.]

---
```

## Section 3: Tool Announcements — New Launches & Major Updates

```markdown
## 3. Tool Announcements — New Launches & Major Updates
```

Comprehensive table (minimum 8 rows):

```markdown
| Tool | Announced By | Date | What It Does | Bill's Relevance |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **[Tool Name]** | @handle | [Date] | [One-sentence description] | **[Critical/High/Medium/Low]** — [brief explanation] |
```

## Section 4: Key Insights & Threads — Best Substantive Content

```markdown
## 4. Key Insights & Threads — Best Substantive Content
```

Feature 3-5 substantive threads:

```markdown
### Thread [Letter]: [Author] (@handle) — "[Thread Topic]"
**Score: [X.X]/10** | [Direct URL](URL) | [Date]

[2-3 paragraph summary of the thread's argument, evidence, and conclusions.]

**Key Points Extracted:**
- [Point 1]
- [Point 2]
- [Point 3]

**Action for Bill:** [Specific recommendation tied to the thread's insights.]

---
```
